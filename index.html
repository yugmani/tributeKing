King Birendra and Queen Aishorya

Our Beloved King and Queen

Late Birendra Bir Bikram Shah was the King of Nepal from 1972 until 2001. The eldest son of King Mahendra, he reigned until his death in the 2001 Nepalese royal massacre.

  • Born: December 28, 1945, Kathmandu, Nepal
  • House:Tenth ruler in the Shah dynasty in Nepal
  • Married:Queen Aishwarya R.L. Rana on February 23, 1970
  • Education: Eton College, Harvard University, The University of Tokyo, Loreto Convent, St. Joseph's School North Point
  • Throne: January 31, 1972
  • Crowned: February 24, 1975.
  • Sons/Daughter: Dipendra Shah, Nirajan Shah and Shruti Rana
  • Assassinated: June 1, 2001, Narayanhiti Palace Museum, Kathmandu, Nepal
